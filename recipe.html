Tips & Secrets
  • 2 cups golden sugar crystals
  • 1 tbsp rainbow sprinkles
  • ½ cup rich cocoa chunks
  • 3 drops of essence of laughter
  • Secret pinch of unicorn salt (shhh!)
  1. Preheat the magical stove to 350°F.
  2. Mix sugar crystals and cocoa in a sparkling bowl.
  3. Fold in essence of laughter carefully.
  4. Add sprinkles while humming your favorite tune.
  5. Bake until golden brown and glowing.
  • Work quickly—speed improves flavor!
  • Share a smile with a friend for extra magical effect.
  • Never stir backwards, it creates a bitter taste.
  • Golden candy glows brighter if baked with care.
